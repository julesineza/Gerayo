# Project Brief: Gerayo – Premium Sober Driver Platform

## 1. Project Overview
**Gerayo** is a high-end mobile platform connecting car owners who are under the influence of alcohol with verified sober drivers. The service allows users to be driven home safely in their own vehicles for a fixed fee, prioritizing safety, trust, and effortless usability.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Safety First:** Verified drivers and 24/7 monitoring.
- **Convenience:** Get home in your own car without leaving it behind.
- **Simplicity:** Optimized for users with potentially impaired judgment (minimal taps, high contrast).
- **Transparency:** Fixed pricing and live tracking.

---

## 2. Target Audience
1.  **Passengers (Car Owners):** Individuals who have consumed alcohol and need a safe way to get themselves and their vehicle home.
2.  **Drivers:** Professional, vetted drivers looking for flexible earnings by providing a vital safety service.

---

## 3. Design Strategy (Gerayo Core)
The design system is engineered for high visibility and low cognitive load.

- **Theme:** Dark mode primary (Deep Navy #0B1F3A) with high-contrast accents.
- **Typography:** Inter (Sans-serif) for maximum legibility.
- **Visual Language:** 
    - 16px rounded corners for a modern, friendly feel.
    - Large touch targets (min 48dp) for ease of use.
    - Premium 3D illustrations to build trust.
    - Clean, professional iconography.
- **Key UX Principle:** The "3-Tap Rule" – reaching the primary goal (requesting a ride) in three taps or fewer.

---

## 4. Feature Roadmap & Screen Inventory

### Phase 1: Foundation & Authentication (Completed)
- **Onboarding:** 3-step sequence emphasizing safety and professional vetting.
- **Login/Sign-up:** Streamlined authentication with social login support.
- **Role Selection:** Clear path for Passenger or Driver registration.

### Phase 2: Passenger Experience (Completed)
- **Home Screen:** "Get Me Home" primary action with auto-location.
- **Booking Flow:** Fixed price display and driver matching.
- **Live Tracking:** Real-time map, driver details, and safety shield status.
- **Trip Completion:** Summary, digital receipt, and rating system.

### Phase 3: Driver Experience (Completed)
- **Dashboard:** Shift toggle (Online/Offline) and earnings overview.
- **Incoming Request:** High-urgency card with pickup/destination and estimated earnings.

### Phase 4: Financials (Completed)
- **Wallet:** Balance management, multiple payment methods (Visa, Apple Pay, M-Pesa), and transaction history.

### Phase 5: Pending Modules (Planned)
- **Driver Registration:** Detailed verification flow (National ID, License, Background check).
- **Settings & Profile:** Personal information and app preferences.
- **Emergency SOS:** Dedicated safety interface.

---

## 5. Technical & Safety Requirements
- **Real-time GPS:** Precise location tracking for both parties.
- **SOS Integration:** One-tap emergency contact and support connection.
- **Payment Gateway:** Support for Credit/Debit, Mobile Money, and Digital Wallets.
- **Identity Verification:** Selfie and document verification for drivers.
